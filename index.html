Vision

With so many years of cumulative experience in teaching math to kids, college students, and adults, I've learnt to treat each student as an individual. There is no one specific formula that can be applied to all students. Every student needs a teaching methodology personalized for them keeping their needs in mind. The end goal is to equip them in a manner where they don't need me anymore! That's done by making them understand the math, not the problem. Once they understand how concepts work, they can figure out the problems by themselves. And that's when they no longer fear math. It is very rewarding to see that look on a student's face when they finally understand things.

An often ignored, critical factor in successful tutoring is listening skills. Very different from patience. In order to help a student out, I first need to hear from them what they think the problem is. A lot of tutors will talk more under the false understanding that they're teaching more. Effective teaching requires stepping back and allowing the student to attempt the problem. If they make a mistake, I give them a chance to try and find it themselves. This practice can be more effective than 30 minutes of teaching time. There are several such techniques I use to help the student fill in any missing gaps in their math knowledge. And at the end of the day, they generally end up not hating math as much as they did before.

As far as experience goes, I started tutoring math in freshman year of college and never looked back. The Director of my math department recruited me to tutor students in math ranging from algebra to Calculus II. Soon after I joined a private education institution and have been there for seven years now. There I worked with students starting at first grade, all the way up to high school. I am currently their senior most instructor. In addition to math, I have developed engineering based robotics and 3D modeling/Printing courses. Along with all of this, I tutor privately both in person, and online. I excel at teaching math starting at basic computation, arithmetic, all the way up to Pre-algebra, Algebra I & II, Geometry, Trigonometry, Pre-calc, and calculus. Let me help you get better at math. Whatever your needs are, I will create a method to meet them in the most effective way. I'm flexible with scheduling, can meet you at a location of your preference (even online).
